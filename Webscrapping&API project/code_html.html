About

Welcome to our European Fund Screener. The idea is simple: have some extra money you would like to invest in a secured fund ? In this website we present to you a list of 0% capital loss European funds. We know that risk and performance apetite changes from a customer to another, and so, we did our research and categorized these funds depending on their risk and performances observed within the last 12months. You would like to directly find the funds of our main asset managers ? You can have instant access to the different funds created by BlackRock, Amundi and other leading entities in the field by directly clicking on the images above ! Don't forget to check our Performance-Risk matrix below to be sure to find the fund that suits you best !
